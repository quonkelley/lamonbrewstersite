About Lamon Brewster

Professional Experience. Secrets of the Trade.

Lamon Brewster is a Retired World Heavyweight Champion, known for his grit, knockout power, and relentless work ethic in the ring. He has shared the ring with the best in the world—and brings that professional experience to every session.

Beyond boxing, Lamon has built Brewster's Place, collaborates with the Nevada Boxing Hall of Fame (NVBHOF), and created The Champion's Brew coffee line. His programs focus on skillset development and a timeless foundation: discipline, technique, and the mind of a champion.

Elite boxing level training and world class fitness—in and out of the ring.
