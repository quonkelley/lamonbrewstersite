About Lamon Brewster

Heavyweight Legacy. Champion Mindset.

Lamon Brewster is a Retired World Heavyweight Champion, known for his grit, knockout power, and relentless work ethic. He has shared the ring with the best in the world and brings that same championship standard to every training session.

Whether you’re stepping into boxing for the first time or sharpening your skills for the professional stage, Lamon translates decades of experience into clear, intense, and technical coaching.

Training with Lamon means more than just pads and conditioning. It’s about discipline, confidence, and learning how to think like a champion—in and out of the ring.
